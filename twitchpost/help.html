# BASIC

- SEE THE STREAM for class IDs. You MUST use !join <class> before you can play.


- Type R, U, L, D (Right, Up, Left, Down) in chat to move. You will move continuously until you provide another order.

- Type S to stop. This cancels all orders.


- Type A, B and C to use your skills. All classes except Thief have a cooldown of 0 turns for their A, 4 for their B and 8 for their C.


- All attacks are shown with []. All attacks oneshot. You can join immediately after dying but will start at 0 kills again.

Good luck.

Also, I only check the first letter of commands.

To avoid the 30 second chat filter, you can type (for example) "Up", "U" or even "ULELELOELM" to trigger a "U" command.

- - Ranger - -

A

Shoot two projectiles two and three units away, the furthest of which will continue moving.

No cooldown.

B

Jump back two units, leaving an attack in the spot jumped away from.

4 turn cooldown.

C

Jump forwards four units then shoot three projectiles at the new position.

8 turn cooldown.

- - Pyromancer - -

No cooldown.

B

8 turn cooldown.
