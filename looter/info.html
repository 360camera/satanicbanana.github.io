Basic game

Gain gold and items from semi-automatically adventuring in areas. Player gains XP by defeating enemies based on their level and gains stats (in a set distribution) by levelling up. XP also grants skill points which are used to learn skills (shown to the right of the battle screen). When the player dies or retires (retirement is simply suicide with a nicer name), the gold and items will be added to the guild's inventory. The player then creates a new character and can equip them with the items gained in previous runs. These items let the new adventurer tackle stronger challenges. As the adventurer has no method of healing aside from potions, which are given at a limited amount at the start of a run, death is an inevitability with the alternative being progressless stagnation. This means that death is the first reset layer.

Gold can be used for "guild upgrades", which enhance aspects such as XP gain, power of adventurer abilities, item drop rates and similar. Most guild upgrades work in the form of multistage "buildings", which are essentially just progressively more expensive multi-level upgrades. For example, the coin gain building could be a Coinery (+0-25), Mint (+25-50), Bank (+50-75) and Exchange (+75-100) based on its level. Usually, at name change milestones, the upgrade's formula will also improve.

Skill tree

The skill tree is split into 6 rows of abilities, each with 50 levels that keep the same skill point cost. The costs of the rows are (1 / 5 / 10 / 100 / 1000 / 10000) skill points per level. Each row will usually be structured in the order (basic / damage / defense / magic) where magic refers to using abilities. This is not a rule, however.

Abilities

Adventurers have 4 abilities which are unlocked by Spell Tomes. They are as follows:

Slash

25% MP

Deal 150% ATK as damage to the enemy.

Fireball

35% MP

Deal 50% of the MP used to cast this spell as damage to the enemy.

Limit Break

100% MP

Gain a bonus which causes you to gain 2x ATK but take 33% more damage. Lasts until the battle ends. Does not stack with itself.

Giga Blast

100% MP

Deal damage to the enemy equal to the sum of 750% ATK and 200% max MP. Deals up to 3x more damage (at 10% HP) based on missing HP.

Ascension

Ascend to gain Heaven Favour and Hell Favour based on gold gained this ascension. These currencies are used to upgrade Angelic and Demonic buildings respectively, all of which are mutually exclusive with their opposite counterparts. The two currencies are used to level their respective buildings, but only one of the two can be enabled at once. The selected buildings to enable can be modified at any time but all changes will only take effect upon the creation of a new adventurer.
